What is GM DIET.?
  • GM Diet is a 7 day only diet plan to quickly lose weight (up to 8kg) for that perfect look boosting your confidenence.
  • Diet consists of fruits, vegetables, dairy and gains that you any way consume in day to day life-just that the diet program organises the quantity and type of food to be taken on each day.
  • Check out sample diet plan for all seven days.

What are benefits of GM Diet?
  • Lose upto 8kgs in just 7 days.
  • Cleanse and detoxify your body - feel energetic and light.
  • Glowing skin.
  • Remove fat from waist belly.

Any side effects?
  • Mild headache : with every diet plan change, your body resists and takes a while to getting used to it. it goes away with your detemination (control your cravings) and as you progress intp the diet.
  • Muscle weakness : This again is a case of your body resisting to new diet during initial days. This too is temporary and gor=es away as you progress into the diet.
  • Drink plenty of water to avoid above two effects.
  • GM diet should not be followed by diabetic person,pregnant woman,breastfeeding mom,thyroid problems and during menstruation.

Tips to maximise weight loss?
  • Drink lots of water (minimum 14 glass)
  • Supplement diet with moderate exercise daily (walk/jog)
  • Only Green tea(without suger / honey) allowed.
  • Strict no to alcohol or cold drink.
  • Give at least 2 week of break before you start GM diet again.
  • Please Note : Weight loss varies (range of 1-8 kgs)for individual based on age,gender,medical condition and strictness towards following diet.

After GM diet

  • To begin with :-
  • Continue eating healthy diet (fruits,vegetables).
  • Start exercise(gyn/yoga).
  • Make drinking water an habit - it does wonders!.
  • No alcohol or no cold drink.
